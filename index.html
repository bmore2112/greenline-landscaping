Family-owned · Local roots

Started with one truck, a wheelbarrow, and a long memory for plants.

Greenline began in 2009 when Dani and Marcus Holloway started caring for a handful of gardens near their home in Florence. Today the family still walks every site, draws every plan, and shows up for the first spring cleanup themselves.

  • Sustainable by default

    Native and pollinator plantings, organic feeding programs, and water-wise irrigation as standard practice.

  • The same faces, every visit

    No rotating subcontractors. You get a small, trained crew who learns your property.

  • Built around your block

    We plant for New England winters and Valley soil, not a generic catalog spec.
